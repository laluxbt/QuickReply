⚡ QuickReply User Guide
QuickReply is a fast, easy-to-use web tool designed to help you quickly find, customize, and copy your saved replies and chat macros.

📲 How to Install QuickReply on Your Phone
You don't need to download this from an app store! You can install it directly from your web browser so it sits on your home screen like a regular app.

For iPhone Users (Safari)
Open the QuickReply web link in Safari.

Tap the Share button (the square icon with an arrow pointing up at the bottom of your screen).

Scroll down the menu and tap Add to Home Screen.

Tap Add in the top-right corner. The QuickReply lightning bolt icon will now be on your home screen!

For Android Users (Chrome)
Open the QuickReply web link in Google Chrome.

Tap the Three Dots (Menu) icon in the top-right corner.

Tap Install app or Add to Home screen.

Confirm by tapping Install or Add. The app is now ready on your home screen!

🚀 How to Use the Tool
📋 Tapping to Copy Messages
Normal Messages: Simply tap anywhere on a macro card. The message copies to your clipboard instantly, and a "Copied to clipboard!" message will flash at the top of your screen. You can now switch to your chat app and paste it.

Messages with {username}: If a message has an orange {username} tag, tapping the card will bring up a pop-up window. Type the customer's name, tap Copy, and the tool automatically inserts their name into the message before copying it to your clipboard.

🔍 Finding Your Replies Quickly
Category Tabs: Use the horizontal scrolling bubbles at the top (like Greeting, Closing, KYC) to quickly filter down your list.

Search Bar: Use the search bar at the very bottom of the screen. You can type in words from the title, the message context, or use its shortcut (e.g., typing /kyc will instantly bring up the KYC templates).

➕ Adding, Editing, and Deleting
To add a new reply, tap the blue + button next to the search bar.

To change or delete an existing reply, use the Edit or Delete buttons located in the bottom-right corner of each card.

💾 Backing Up Your Replies (Phone Storage)
Your replies are saved directly inside your phone's web browser cache so they stay there when you close the app. However, if you clear your browser history, they might be lost. Use the buttons in the top right header to protect your data:

Backup: Tap this to download a small backup file directly to your phone's storage. Do this whenever you make important updates to your replies!

Import: If you switch to a new phone or need to restore your list, tap Import and select your saved backup file.

Reset: Tap this if you want to wipe out your custom list and start over with the original factory default messages.
